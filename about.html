About Us

A specialist data consultancy born from hands-on experience in mining and resources. We bridge the gap between raw operational data and smart business decisions.

Our Story

Built From the Ground Up — Literally

BusinessVault Solutions was founded with a clear frustration in mind: mining companies generate enormous volumes of data every day — from assay results to shift reports — but too often that data sits in disconnected spreadsheets, siloed systems, or simply gets lost.

We set out to change that. By combining domain expertise in mining operations with modern data engineering practices, we help resource companies unlock the full value of their data — whether it's a single operation or a multi-site enterprise.

Our approach is practical, transparent, and built around your team's real workflow. We don't just build systems — we ensure your people can use and maintain them.
